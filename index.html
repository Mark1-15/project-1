Who we are

Everyday people, we are members of the community and who have jobs, ffriends, families, and pretty much everything else you have and do but with one difference, that is, we are Christians. And we seek to spread the good news of our Lord Jesus Christ.

What we do

We enjoy fellowship with each other, and the community. We host various different types of activities, to create a safe and welcoming environment for all to enjoy. As well as this we wish to provide hope to this work by making the person of Christ known.
