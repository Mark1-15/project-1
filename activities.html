Coffee Morning

Join us at 10am every Wednesday morning for our weekly coffee morning that's on every week excluding bank holidays. Join us for a chat, meet others in the community while afterwards we sing hymns and learn more about the Christian faith.

Gospel Meeting

At 4:30pm every Sunday afternoon we gather in the church to hear the preaching from Bible where we'll enjoy refreshments and a chat afterwards.

Toddler Group

Join us, and the rest of the toddlers in the community for our toddlers group at 10am every Monday morning to play, learn and sing. Suitable for ages 2-6

Youth Group

After a log week at school, our youth group is a great way to unwind and relax, or wind up and get excited! Games, food and fun await school children age 11-16 who want to join every Friday night at 7pm.

Cost and safety information

As this is a charitable organisation, all activities are completely free of charge! We simply ask you to fill out a booking form that will allow us to gauge the guest count to prepare for the activity to suit all attendees. All refreshments provided has been verified with a 5 star food safety hygiene rating for this organisation.
